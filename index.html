About Bestwise

Leading the Electric Revolution

Best WiseMyth Company Limited is at the forefront of sustainable transportation solutions in Tanzania. We are committed to providing high-quality electric motorcycles that reduce carbon emissions while delivering exceptional performance.

With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we develop models that put money back in your pocket. Our electric motorcycles cost a fraction of a gasoline bike to charge, offering incredible savings on fuel. Riders also benefit from simpler maintenance with no need for oil changes or tune-ups. This reliable, affordable performance, combined with zero emissions, provides a powerful and practical solution for Tanzania's commuters while protecting our local environment.

Located in Dar es Salaam, we serve customers across Tanzania with reliable and efficient electric mobility solutions.
